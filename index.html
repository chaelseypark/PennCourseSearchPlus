Penn Course Search Plus

Get Started

An advanced version of the open‑source extension, Penn Course Search.

AI Course Selection 🛡️

Select your courses and ask ChatGPT to analyze which course you should take.

Checkbox Course Selection UI with Persistent History🚀

Reliable, confirmable way to stage courses for comparison across sessions.

Visual Course Comparison Panel📈

Side‑by‑side metrics view (difficulty, workload, quality) in both table and radar view.

AI course summary and course comparison 🦉

Asks ChatGPT to analyze a course summary and compare courses you select.

Saveable Notes ✨

Attach personal notes to any selected course. Notes persist across sessions for saved selections and are editable inline. Perfect for tracking thoughts, reminders, or planning decisions.

Easy-to-view Prerequisites 🧰

Quickly see prerequisite requirements for each course without digging through the full course description. Clean, direct, and right where you need it.
